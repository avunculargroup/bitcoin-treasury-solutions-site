Why would you consider a small allocation of bitcoin in your treasury?

Hedge Against Inflation

Bitcoin's fixed supply of 21 million coins offers protection against fiat currency devaluation, which is critical given Australia's rising inflation rates (e.g., 3.5% in 2024 per RBA forecasts).

Diversification

Adding Bitcoin to a treasury reduces reliance on traditional assets like cash or bonds, which may underperform in volatile markets, enhancing portfolio resilience.

Global Accessibility

Bitcoin enables seamless, low-cost cross-border transactions, ideal for Australian businesses with international suppliers or customers, avoiding high forex fees.

Attracting Investment

Holding Bitcoin signals innovation, appealing to crypto-savvy investors and customers, especially in tech-driven sectors, boosting brand perception.

Long-Term Growth Potential

Despite volatility, Bitcoin's historical price appreciation (e.g., ~20% annualized returns since 2013) offers potential for significant capital gains over time.

About Us

We are a small and passionate team with deep expertise in Bitcoin, dedicated to educating others about its transformative potential. With years of firsthand experience using and studying Bitcoin, we understand its core properties—decentralisation, scarcity, and censorship resistance—and are committed to communicating its value clearly and effectively.

Our mission is to empower individuals and businesses to embrace Bitcoin as a tool for financial sovereignty. Whether you're new to Bitcoin or an experienced user, we provide insights, resources, and practical guidance to help you navigate this revolutionary technology.

Driven by a belief in Bitcoin's ability to reshape the global financial system, we focus on education, adoption, and real-world use cases. Trust our experience—we've been where you are, and we're here to help you succeed in your Bitcoin journey.
